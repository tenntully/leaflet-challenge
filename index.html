Background

This site and application is being provided to analyze United States Geological Survey (USGS) measured earthquake data. The data is provided by USGS in GeoJSON format (https://earthquake.usgs.gov/earthquakes/feed/v1.0/geojson.php). This application pulls this data and plots it along with relevant details including earthquake location, magnitude, and depth.
